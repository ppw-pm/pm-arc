I'm Pornpawee Manyuen — a UX designer with a deep love for the space where human language meets technology. My background in linguistics gives me a unique lens for understanding how people communicate and interact with systems.

After my Master's in HCID, I've worked across industries — from Southeast Asia's largest OTA to a global social media platform — crafting experiences that are as intuitive as they are meaningful.

I believe in research-grounded design: listening carefully, testing often, and always advocating for the person on the other side of the screen.
